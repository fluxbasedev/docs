## What is Fluxbase?

Fluxbase is a bot listing platform for [Fluxer](https://fluxer.app) - a place where bot developers can list their bots and server owners can discover new ones for their communities.

## How it works

Bot developers can submit their bots to Fluxbase, where they go through a review process before being listed publicly. Once approved, your bot is visible to the entire Fluxbase community and can be discovered by server owners looking for bots just like yours.

## Getting started

If you're a bot developer looking to list your bot, head over to the [Listing your first bot](/listing-your-first-bot) guide to get started.

If you're looking to integrate with the Fluxbase API, check out the [Authentication](/api/authentication) guide.

## Need help?

If you run into any issues or have questions, you can reach us through the official [Fluxbase Support Server](https://fluxer.gg/fluxbase) or submit a [bug report](https://fluxbase.dev/bug-report) directly from the dashboard.
